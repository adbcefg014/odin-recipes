Easy Fried Cabbage

Description

  • 2 teaspoon grapeseed oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, cjopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  1. Heat grapeseed oil in large cast iron skillet over medium head
  2. Add onions and cook until softened
  3. Add garlic, stir and cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes
